Where She's From is a small, simple hack-and-slash game exclusively for Arduboy.

A strangely quiet yet ruthless outlaw has shown up on the frontier.
Though there have been others like her before, none have been as efficient.

View repository and download the game here.

How to Play

CONTROLS
Left & Right: Run
A: Solar Dodge
B: Solar Slash
Up + B: Breaker Slash
Down + B: Cleaving Slash


GOAL
She simply has to slay every enemy on the field.

TIME
She has seven minutes to complete each mission.
If the time reaches 7:00 PM, she will die.
Taking damage will immediately advance time, so be careful.

SOLAR DODGE
While using her Solar Dodge, she will be impervious to enemy attacks.
However, constantly using her Solar Dodge will exhaust her, and leave her open.
If she times her Solar Dodge right before an enemy attack, she will go into Quickdraw for 3 seconds.
Quickdraw will allow her to move at light speed and rapidly attack enemies without caution.

SOLAR SLASH
Her most basic attack may do the least damage and have no special properties, but it is her quickest attack.
She can use this to deal more damage against a vulnerable enemy.

BREAKER SLASH
Though this attack is fairly week, it is a great supporting tool.
If she uses Breaker Slash on an enemy who is blocking, she will temporarily break their guard.
She can also use Breaker Slash against dynamite sticks to reflect them.

CLEAVING SLASH
This attack may appear strong, but it is slower than her other attacks.
If she uses this against an enemy who is preparing an attack, she will Clash with them.
While clashing, rotating the D-Pad clockwise will allow her to overpower her enemy and deliver a heavy blow to them.
However, she will be unable to Clash for 5 seconds afterwards (indicated by a pocket watch icon in the top right of the screen).
